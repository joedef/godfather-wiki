## Caveats

**Box office from the 1970s is unreliable.** Studios reported *rentals* (the distributor's share) rather than gross, the two get mixed together in later citations, and *The Godfather* has been reissued repeatedly. Any single number for the first film is a guess. See [Box Office](box-office.md).

**Runtimes vary by print and territory.** PAL speed-up shortens European releases by about 4%; the 1991 *Part III* home video cut runs ~8 minutes longer than theatrical. Figures here are US theatrical.

**In-universe dates are reconstructed.** The films rarely date themselves explicitly, and the novel and the continuation novels sometimes contradict them. The [Timeline](timeline.md) flags where a date is inferred rather than stated.
