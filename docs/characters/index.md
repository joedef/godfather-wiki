# Characters

<p class="epigraph">A man who doesn't spend time with his family can never be a real man.
<cite>Vito Corleone — a maxim the film spends three hours dismantling</cite></p>

Every significant figure across the novel, the trilogy, and the continuation novels. Where a character differs meaningfully between versions, the page says so. Nothing here is spoiler-protected.

**Start with the [Family Tree](family-tree.md)** if you've lost the thread.

---

## The Corleone family

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **[Vito Corleone](vito-corleone.md)**

    ---

    Born Vito Andolini, Corleone, Sicily, 1891. Orphaned at nine. Founder. The only man in the trilogy who dies happy.

-   **[Michael Corleone](michael-corleone.md)**

    ---

    The war hero who was going to be a senator. The trilogy is the record of what happened instead.

-   **[Sonny Corleone](sonny-corleone.md)**

    ---

    Eldest son, acting Don, and the most competent man in the family at everything except judgment.

-   **[Fredo Corleone](fredo-corleone.md)**

    ---

    The middle son. Passed over, humiliated, and — for exactly one afternoon — dangerous.

-   **[Tom Hagen](tom-hagen.md)**

    ---

    German-Irish orphan, adopted at twelve, consigliere at thirty. Not a Sicilian, and never allowed to forget it.

-   **[Connie Corleone](connie-corleone.md)**

    ---

    The daughter. Beaten, widowed by her own brother, and eventually the coldest Corleone alive.

-   **[Kay Adams](kay-adams.md)**

    ---

    The New Hampshire schoolteacher who married in. The audience's proxy, and the one person Michael lies to directly.

-   **[Carmela Corleone](carmela-corleone.md)**

    ---

    Vito's wife. Says almost nothing across two films and holds the entire household together.

-   **[Apollonia Vitelli](apollonia-vitelli.md)**

    ---

    Michael's first wife, married in Sicily and killed by a bomb meant for him.

-   **[Vincent Mancini](vincent-mancini.md)**

    ---

    Sonny's illegitimate son. Inherits the family in *Part III*, and the temper along with it.

-   **[Mary Corleone](mary-corleone.md)**

    ---

    Michael's daughter. The price, finally, made explicit.

</div>

## The organization

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **[Clemenza & Tessio](clemenza-and-tessio.md)** — the two original capos. One takes the cannoli; the other asks for a pass, for old times' sake.
-   **[Luca Brasi](luca-brasi.md)** — the family's most feared soldier, who sleeps with the fishes in the first act.
-   **[Frank Pentangeli](frank-pentangeli.md)** — Clemenza's successor, and the man who nearly brings the whole thing down in a Senate hearing room.
-   **[Johnny Fontane](johnny-fontane.md)** — the godson. A singer, a subplot, and a rumor that will not die.
-   **[Minor Characters](minor-characters.md)** — Neri, Rocco, Cicci, Bonasera, Tommasino, Genco, Enzo, and everyone else.

</div>

## The opposition

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **[Sollozzo & McCluskey](sollozzo-and-mccluskey.md)** — the narcotics man and the bought cop. Michael's first two kills.
-   **[Barzini & the Five Families](barzini-and-the-five-families.md)** — the quiet one who was behind everything, and the New York map.
-   **[Carlo Rizzi](carlo-rizzi.md)** — the brother-in-law who sold Sonny for a phone call.
-   **[Moe Greene](moe-greene.md)** — the man who built Las Vegas and got shot through the eye for it.
-   **[Hyman Roth](hyman-roth.md)** — *Part II*'s antagonist, and the only person who ever genuinely out-thinks Michael.
-   **[Zasa, Altobello & Lucchesi](zasa-altobello-lucchesi.md)** — the *Part III* conspiracy, from street-level to the Italian state.

</div>
