## Brando and the Oscar refusal

<div class="video">
  <iframe src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/UBE19iiZe84"
          title="Marlon Brando on Sacheen Littlefeather, Dick Cavett Show"
          loading="lazy" allowfullscreen
          allow="accelerometer; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; picture-in-picture; web-share"
          referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin"></iframe>
</div>

**[Brando](../people/marlon-brando.md) explains the refusal.** His own account of sending someone in his place to decline Best Actor, and why. Whatever you make of the gesture, this is Brando articulate and unguarded, which is rarer on film than his acting is. — *The Dick Cavett Show*
{ .video-caption }

<div class="video">
  <iframe src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/Zi_N1NDmSM8"
          title="Sacheen Littlefeather reads the speech she was not allowed to give"
          loading="lazy" allowfullscreen
          allow="accelerometer; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; picture-in-picture; web-share"
          referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin"></iframe>
</div>

**Sacheen Littlefeather reads Brando's full 1973 statement.** She was given sixty seconds on the night and improvised; the prepared text ran to pages. The Academy formally apologized to her in 2022, shortly before her death. Her claimed Apache heritage was disputed by her own family after she died — a controversy that remains unresolved and that the Academy has not revisited.
{ .video-caption }
