## A standing warning about this material

*Godfather* production history is **unusually unreliable**, and it's worth understanding why before you trust any anecdote about it.

The stories have been retold for fifty years by participants with strong incentives to improve them. [Robert Evans](../people/robert-evans.md) and [Coppola](../people/francis-ford-coppola.md) spent decades publicly disputing who saved the film. [Albert S. Ruddy](../people/albert-s-ruddy.md)'s account is the basis for a television series he executive-produced. Gianni Russo has claimed a great deal that nobody can corroborate. And the mob-adjacent stories in particular were always partly unverifiable and are now entirely so, because everyone who could confirm them is dead.

This wiki's practice: **when a story is disputed, it says so and names who claims what.** If you find a page stating a contested anecdote as fact, that's a bug — please [open an issue](https://github.com/joedef/godfather-wiki/issues).

The most reliable single account of the production is **Mark Seal's *Leave the Gun, Take the Cannoli* (2021)**, which is journalism rather than memoir. See [Sources](sources.md).
